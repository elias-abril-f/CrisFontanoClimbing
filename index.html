About Me

I started climbing in 2009 and over the past 15 years I have travelled around the world bouldering and sport climbing

Since stepping into the coaching world in 2018, I've worked with a range of climbers — from beginners to athletes competing at national level. In 2022, I earned my Development Coach Certification, and I continue to coach Team GB athletes to this day.

In my own climbing, I've bouldered up to 7C+/V10, redpointed 8a, and onsighted over 100 routes in the 7th grade. Whether on rock or in the gym, I'm always psyched to share what I have learned and help others grow as climbers.

"If you don't let go, you can't fall off" — Jerry Moffatt
